What We Do

Chisipite One Stop Solar Shop is a company that provides solar solutions to modern day electrical problems to all in need.

Our Values

CONSISTENT RELIABILITY

Chisipite One Stop Solar is committed to being consistently reliable in its customer service, product quality, stock holdings, pricing, warranty terms as well as after sales support. We strongly believe this is what sets us apart as a cutting edge renewable energy service provider. We look forward to becoming your green energy partner.

SERVICE WARRANTY

Chisipite One Stop Solar prides itself in offering world class warranty terms. Warranties on our equipment range from five to twenty-five years depending on the type of equipment selected. All of our warranties are backed by manufacturers with whom we have exceptional relationships. In order to qualify for our warranty, we require a One Stop Solar Accredited Installer to inspect and commission the equipment.

TECHNICAL BACKUP

Chisipite One Stop Solar has invested heavily in a fully-equipped workshop facility that enables strong technical support. All our equipment allows online monitoring enabling customers, installers and distributors to respond and troubleshoot from remote locations. On the rare occasions where equipment requires repair or servicing, Chisipite One Stop Solar carry spares and are authorized to repair or replace components under warranty.

AFTER SALES SUPPORT

Given the fact that the equipment we supply is of such high calibre, Chisipite One Stop Solar strongly believe that your investment requires world class after sales support. Your accredited installer, also supported by ourselves, is your first port of call. Should occasion then demand, our door is always open and we welcome your interaction and feedback.

NATURE OF PRODUCT

A world class reputation can only be built on world class products backed by world class service and personnel. Chisipite One Stop Solar have carefully selected equipment that typically ranks in the top four worldwide. We have been appointed as preferred distributors for these products in Zimbabwe.

LARGE STOCK HOLDINGS

Chisipite One Stop Solar carries large holdings of stock. This ensures that when a client is ready to invest, there is no lead time involved in receiving product. It also enables us to honour our warranties in the quickest possible time.

REAL TIME MONITORING

All our equipment facilitates online real-time monitoring, providing there is internet availability. Chisipite One Stop Solar work closely with the installers to assist clients in monitoring their systems. The advantages and benefits of this type of service ensure a better Return on Investment.

ESTABLISHING COMPETENCY

The renewable energy sector is by its very nature a long-term investment with high return value. Chisipite One Stop Solar strongly believes in supporting local installers as well as equipping the next generation. We accomplish this by hosting regular training sessions and active mentorship programs. On occasion, we are also open to facilitating in-house training for organizations with existing competent staff.
