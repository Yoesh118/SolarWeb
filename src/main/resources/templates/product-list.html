UP 2500 24V 100AH

The Pylotech UP2500 is a Hybrid Energy Storage System (HESS) provided by Pylontech. Pylontech uses its own lithium iron phosphate cells to ensure the highest safety value. An inbuilt Battery Management System protects pylontech battery cells from abnormal electrical conditions like charging and discharging. We provide you with a 7 year warranty on purchased batteries.

USC3000 48V 75AH

The Pylontech USC3000 3.55kWh lithium-ion battery is made up of lithium-ion modules. These allow us to have a modular photovoltaic storage system to expand its capacity with up to 16 units without the need for any additional equipment. It contains an internal BMS that is responsible for distributing power between the cells efficiently and safely, preventing overcharging or excessive discharging. All clients that purchase this battery are rewarded with a 7 year warranty on the same.

UP5000 48V 100AH

The Pylontech Lithium ion UP5000 Solar battery is made up of lithium-ion modules. This battery is a great option when one needs to support high power applications, limited installation space, restrictedload-bearing and long life cycle. This lithium battery has an easily workable configuration that allows multiple batteries to be connected in parallel. All clients that purchase this battery are rewarded with a 7 year warranty on the same.
